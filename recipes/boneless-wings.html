Ingredients List

  • Oil
  • FLour
  • Salt
  • Chicken Breast
  • Cayenne
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in large bowl
  2. Whisk together egg and milk in separate bowl
  3. Dip chicken in egg mixture, then roll in flower mixture. Repeat so each piece is double coated
  4. Refrigerate coated chicken for 20 mins
  5. Fry chick in oil
